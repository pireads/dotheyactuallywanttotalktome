I have this habit of texting people who reply much less frequently than I text them.
I try very hard not to take it personally, but unconsciously it's hard to get around sometimes. I just don't want to bother people who don't want to be bothered.
I bought this domain with the intention of creating a website where I could type in someone's name and find out whether they actually wanted to talk to me.
I quickly realized that, while I'm pretty good with computers, I'm not that good.
Yet.

But I think this website will still be useful in its current form as a collection of opinions on the topic from people that I respect.
That way, when I inevitably have this question again I can still come back here and hopefully get some perspective.
