I have this habit of texting people who reply much less frequently than I text them.
I try very hard not to take it personally, but unconsciously it's hard to get around sometimes. I just don't want to bother people who don't want to be bothered.
I bought this domain with the intention of creating a website where I could type in someone's name and find out whether they actually wanted to talk to me.
I quickly realized that, while I'm pretty good with computers, I'm not that good.

Yet.

But I think this website will still be useful in its current form as a collection of opinions on the topic.
That way, when I inevitably have this question again I can still come back here and hopefully get some perspective.

If you're reading this and you're not me but you think this website was at least partially written about you, you should know that I very much agree with Dodie on this one.
I'm well aware that there are many reasons that someone might have for taking a while to respond to their texts that don't involve whether or not they hate me.
But also if you think I might have had you in mind when I wrote this site and you actually don't want to hear from me, you should probably tell me.
That way this site can serve its original purpose in at least some capacity.

Conclusion

Dear Future Avi,

People will reply to you when they feel like replying to you, and oftentimes that decision will have absolutley nothing to do with how they feel about you.
Maybe this wouldn't be an issue if you didn't derive so much of your self worth based on other people's perceived opinions of you.
Avi from the past clearly wants to talk to you, and that should be enough.

Or maybe you've already outgrown whatever this is, and you've come here to laugh at how fragile and insecure you used to be.
Or you've figured out how to make this page solve our original problem and just tell you whether they actually want to talk to you.

In either case, congratulations on the personal growth.
